Three.js Inspector Labs

ThreeInspector is set of small tools for Three.js, in the form of bookmarklets. Until it becomes mature enough for Three.js or gets its own repository, this page serves as a temporary shelter for these experiments. To use drag the bookmarklets into your toolbar (done once only as updates to ThreeInspector are automatic). To run, click on the bookmarklet inside three.js pages.

Three.js Scene Inspector - Displays objects found in Three.js Scenes. Drag the values to alter properties of objects.
